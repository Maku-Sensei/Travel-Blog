Hello and Welcome to Sensei's Guide to Tourism in Kivotos. For those who has yet to know me, I am Maku-Sensei of Schale and I occasionally do travel blog videos for my students to see publicly in my main channel, so that they get to know the culture, environment, life and wonders in other academy districts.

I conduct travel blog videos with students who wanna spend time with me. We get to experience different kinds of food and tourist attractions that the academies offer, we get to see other wonders like the Tree of Life in Hyakkiyako Alliance Academy and the massive well-maintained gardens in Trinity Academy. We travel far and wide within the districts we visit using the official Schale-Bus. While watching our blogs that we make, we hope you get to see how magnificent all the academies are in Kivotos.

If you have questions you wanna ask or suggestions for places you wanna see, please go to the Contacts Tab located at the Navigation bar or email us directly at schalesensei_travelblog@kivotos.com. We hope you enjoy our videos and have a good day!
