Shiratori District is one of the most populated districts in Kivotos, many administrative faiclities are located here including the Sanctum Tower. Many tourist destinations have been built to attract students from all Kivotos and its various academies. One of the locations in particular which is the Shiratori District Amusement Park is a shining beacon of mixed culture and enjoyment, many students come here to spend time with each other despite their difference in academic affiliations.
